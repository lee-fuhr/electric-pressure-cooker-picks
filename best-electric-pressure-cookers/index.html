Best for small kitchens

When counter space is at a premium, an appliance that can pressure cook and air fry in one unit is a lifesaver. The Ninja Foodi 9-in-1 does both without requiring extra gadgets. It replaces a pressure cooker and an air fryer (and often a slow cooker too), making it the most space-efficient choice for apartment cooks. Our pick: Ninja Foodi 9-in-1 Pressure Cooker and Air Fryer.

Best for pressure cooking enthusiasts

Our verdict

For most households, the Instant Pot Duo 7-in-1 remains the best balance of affordability, reliability, and ease of use. If you have room in your budget and need air frying, the Ninja Foodi is a worthy splurge, while the Breville Fast Slow Pro is the ultimate choice for cooks who want full control. Any of these electric pressure cookers will save you time and expand your cooking repertoire, so choose based on your priorities and enjoy the convenience.
